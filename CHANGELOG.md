### Recipe Format
All 100 recipes follow a consistent 17-section structure including:
- Problem/Outcome framing
- Good Fit/Not Good Fit guidance
- Prerequisites checklist
- Claude capabilities and human judgment sections
- Step-by-step implementation with prompts
- Example outputs
- Troubleshooting tables
- Tips from experience
- Variations
- Building repeatable systems
